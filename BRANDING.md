# CYS Accountants — Brand Guidelines

## Colours

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Navy (Primary) | `#202a44` | Header, footer, contact info panel, service icons |
| Teal (Accent) | `#54dec5` | CTA buttons, hover states, section underlines, icon highlights |
| White | `#ffffff` | Body text on dark backgrounds, form backgrounds |
| Dark Text | `#1f252b` | Body copy on light backgrounds |
| Mid Grey | `#555555` | Secondary body copy |
| Light Grey | `#f5f5f5` | Contact section background |
| Black | `#000000` | Scroll-to-top button |

**Colour philosophy:** Dark navy dominates structural elements (header, footer, info panels). Teal is used sparingly as an accent to draw attention to interactive elements and key headings. The palette is professional, trustworthy, and conservative — appropriate for a financial services business.

---

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Headings / Navigation / Buttons | Montserrat | 400, 700 | Sans-serif, uppercase with letter-spacing |
| Body Copy | Roboto | 300, 400, 500, 700 | Clean, highly legible |
| Fallback | Old Standard TT | 400 | Serif fallback (loaded but rarely dominant) |

**Type treatment:**
- Section titles: uppercase, letter-spacing ~2px, navy colour, teal underline bar
- Hero title: uppercase, letter-spacing ~3px, white, large display size
- Navigation links: uppercase, letter-spacing 0.5px, 0.85rem, white
- Body text: sentence case, 0.92–0.98rem, relaxed line-height (1.6–1.75)

---

## Logo

- **File:** `assets/cysaccounts_logo.png`
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Usage:** Displayed white/light on the dark navy header background
- **Max height in header:** ~60px
- **Clear space:** Maintain padding around the logo; do not place on busy backgrounds without an overlay

---

## Iconography

- **Library:** Font Awesome 5 (Free tier)
- **Style:** Mix of regular (`far`) and solid (`fas`) variants
- **Icons used:**
  - `far fa-bookmark` — Business Advisory
  - `far fa-user` — Income Tax Return
  - `far fa-building` — Corporate Compliance
  - `fas fa-pencil-alt` — Bookkeeping
  - `fas fa-map-marker-alt` — Address
  - `fas fa-map-pin` — Postal Address
  - `fas fa-phone` — Phone
  - `far fa-envelope` — Email
  - `far fa-clock` — Business Hours
- **Icon boxes:** Square, navy background, white icon, 56×56px

---

## Buttons & Interactive Elements

| Element | Style |
|---|---|
| Primary CTA (Hero) | Transparent fill, 2px teal border, teal text → teal background / navy text on hover |
| Form Submit | Navy background, white text, uppercase Montserrat → darker navy on hover |
| Nav links | No border, hover: subtle teal tint background + teal text |

**Border radius:** 3px across all interactive elements — slightly rounded but not pill-shaped.

---

## Layout & Spacing

- **Max content width:** 1140px (centred, 20px side padding)
- **Section padding:** 80px top/bottom on main sections
- **Grid:** 2-column for services (collapses to 1 on mobile), 3-column for credentials, 2:1 split for contact
- **Breakpoints:**
  - `≤ 900px` — single column layout for services and credentials
  - `≤ 700px` — mobile navigation (hamburger), stacked contact grid
  - `≤ 480px` — service items stack icon above text

---

## Imagery Style

- **Hero:** Full-bleed background images of professional/office settings with a dark navy overlay (~62% opacity) to maintain text legibility
- **Slideshow:** 4 images cycling every 5 seconds with a 0.8s fade transition
- **Credential images:** 600×400px, 3:2 ratio, photographs of certification/partner logos
- **Tone:** Professional, clean, trustworthy — no illustrations or abstract graphics

---

## Voice & Copy Tone

- Professional but approachable ("friendly, experienced")
- Concise service descriptions
- Emphasis on qualifications, compliance expertise, and local presence (Gold Coast / Brisbane)
- All-caps used for section headings and CTAs only
- Business name always written: **CYS Accountants** (not CYS ACCOUNTANTS in body copy)
