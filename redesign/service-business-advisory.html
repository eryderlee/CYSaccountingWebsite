What We Do

Business Advisory
& Tax Compliance

"GST, BAS, payroll, super — the compliance list never ends. We keep you on the right side of the ATO so you can focus on running your business."

Running a business means dealing with GST, BAS, payroll, superannuation and more — all with strict ATO deadlines attached. Our business advisory service handles your entire compliance picture, keeping your books clean, your obligations met, and your business out of trouble with the tax office. Whether you've just started out or you've been operating for years, we make sure nothing slips through the cracks.

What's Included

GST Registration & Lodgement

We register your business for GST and handle all the ongoing lodgement requirements, so the ATO always has current information.

BAS Preparation & Lodgement

We prepare and lodge your Business Activity Statement on time, every quarter — accurately calculating what you owe or are owed.

Payroll Processing & STP

We manage your payroll and submit Single Touch Payroll data directly to the ATO each pay run — keeping you compliant with every pay cycle.

PAYG Withholding

We calculate and manage PAYG withholding from employee wages so the right amounts are held back and reported to the ATO correctly.

Superannuation Compliance

We make sure your super guarantee contributions are calculated correctly and paid on time — avoiding the ATO's steep penalty rates for late payments.

Financial Reporting & Analysis

We produce clear profit & loss reports and balance sheets so you always know how your business is actually performing — not just guessing.

Business Structure Advice

Sole trader, company, trust or partnership — we help you choose and review the right structure for your tax position and growth plans.

ATO Correspondence & Audit Support

If the ATO contacts you or selects your business for review, we handle all correspondence and represent your interests throughout the process.

Who It Suits

Whether you're a sole trader just getting started or a growing company with staff and complex reporting requirements, we've got you covered. If you've ever missed a BAS deadline, received an ATO letter, or simply aren't sure whether you're meeting your obligations — this service is built for you.

Frequently Asked

Start with a free phone or in-person chat with our team. Our BAS preparation fees vary depending on the complexity of your business activity. We offer transparent, fixed-fee pricing — contact us for a tailored quote. Generally speaking, most small business BAS lodgements start from a very affordable flat fee with no hidden charges.
Most businesses lodge BAS quarterly, with due dates generally one month after the end of each quarter (28 October, 28 February, 28 April, 28 July). Monthly lodgers have different due dates. Our team tracks all deadlines for you so nothing slips through the cracks.
You must register for GST if your annual turnover is $75,000 or more (or $150,000 for non-profit organisations). Even if your turnover is below this threshold, voluntary registration can be beneficial in some circumstances. We'll help you make the right decision for your situation.
Yes — we regularly assist clients who have fallen behind on their BAS or tax lodgements. We can liaise with the ATO on your behalf and put a plan in place to get you back on track while minimising penalties where possible. Don't delay — the sooner you act, the better the outcome.
A BAS (Business Activity Statement) is lodged regularly throughout the year — usually quarterly — and reports GST, PAYG withholding and other obligations. A tax return is lodged once a year and calculates your total taxable income and the tax you owe for that financial year. Both are important, and most businesses need to do both. We handle each at the right time so you don't have to keep track.
